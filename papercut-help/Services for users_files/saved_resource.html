                  Matthew Lee (PaperCut) Mod Jobi Varughese • 9 months ago

                  Hi Jobi!

                  Great question. If you are looking for PaperCut NG/MF to help with the deployment of printers to your users, then we have our Print Deploy solution available to help with this. Through a client installed on each user's computer you can automatically install the print queues they should have available, as well as defining which printer is the default.

                  If you are aleady deploying queues through another method, for example, Group Policy, then this default printer management would be handled through the settings that product/feature offers.

                  If you'd like to discuss your specific setup in more detail please reach out to our support team https://support.papercut.com
